Border Crossing Entry Data

Our team developed this website

https://data.bts.gov/Research-and-Statistics/Border-Crossing-Entry-Data/keg4-3bc2/data

Inspiration

We discovered a dataset of "Border Crossing Entry Data" for people coming into the United States from Mexico and Canada on the Kaggle Website. Upon further review, we found the original dataset on the U.S.Bureau of Transportation's website. The data collection was immense and included Latitude and Longitude columns. This was important to us since we wanted to create a map-based visualization of the data.
